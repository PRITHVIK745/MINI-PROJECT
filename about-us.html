Welcome to Our College

Welcome to Mysuru Royal Institute of Technology (MRIT), an esteemed engineering college committed to providing world-class education and fostering innovation. Located in the heritage city of Mysuru, Karnataka, MRIT is dedicated to shaping the next generation of engineers, leaders, and innovators who will contribute to society's progress.

The mission of Mysuru Royal Institute of Technology (MRIT) is to provide high-quality education in engineering and technology while ensuring holistic development. We aim to develop individuals who not only excel academically but also contribute to society through leadership, creativity, and innovation. MRIT strives to instill strong values and ethics that will guide students to make a positive impact on the world.

Mysuru Royal Institute of Technology (MRIT) was established in [Year] with the goal of providing top-tier engineering education to students in Karnataka and beyond. Over the years, MRIT has consistently focused on academic excellence, skill development, and research initiatives to prepare students for the ever-changing technological landscape. The college has rapidly grown into one of the leading institutions in the region, offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines.

Academic Programs

MRIT offers a wide variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, each designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to excel in their careers. Some of our core programs include:

  • Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Computer Science Engineering

  • Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Civil Engineering

  • Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Electronics and Communication Engineering

  • Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Mechanical Engineering

  • Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Information Science

    • In addition to these, MRIT also offers postgraduate programs in engineering, aimed at deepening students' expertise in their chosen fields.
